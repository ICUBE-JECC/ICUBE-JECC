icube

A state level science project competition for polytechnic and higher secondary school students. This Innovative project is one of the ways in which the students can exhibit their creative skills and show entrepreneurial capabilities.
  It is an endeavour to enhance the culture of scientific inquiry, entrepreneurship and its relevance among student community. This contest is more concerned in providing socially relevant project. Student project competitions are events focusing on the development and presentation of fresh solutions that can relate to any discipline from arts to engineering. By supporting and accelerating their talents, students can reach new academic heights.

OBJECTIVES OF icube:

  • • To create a culture of innovation in an educational institute.
  • • To develop entrepreneurial mindset among students.
  • • To make students aware of latest industry trends.
  • • To make students aware of government support available to them to carry forward their projects as entrepreneurial ventures.
  • • To connect with like minded innovators for further enhancement of the project.

GUIDELINES

Students have to register for the event and shortlisted students will be invited to participate in the semi-final event.
Each team will be provided with a mentor to aid in the completion of the project.
The semi-final round will be assessed by a JEC evaluation committee. We will invite external experts to evaluate the final round.
The semi-final and the grand finale will be scheduled in the month of January 2022.
